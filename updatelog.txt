v0.6 — 2026-05-11  (Bug fix)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIXED
  - Flow diagram incorrectly showed "Solar charge" + solar→battery arrow when
    solar and battery were both powering home (battery discharging)
  - Root cause: GoodWe reports battery_w as an unsigned magnitude; the old
    sign-based check (batW > 20 = charging, batW < -20 = discharging) always
    treated an active battery as charging
  - Fix: battery direction now inferred from energy balance —
    bat_net = load_w − pv_w − grid_contribution; positive → discharging,
    negative → charging; correct regardless of API sign convention
